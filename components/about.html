About LuxBath

Crafting Exceptional Bathroom Experiences Since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by interior designer Sarah Johnson, LuxBath emerged from a passion for creating extraordinary bathroom spaces that combine functionality with unparalleled elegance.

What began as a boutique showroom in Montreal has evolved into North America's premier destination for luxury bathroom accessories, serving discerning homeowners, interior designers, and architects.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, unwavering commitment to quality, and a deep understanding of how the right accessories can transform a bathroom into a personal sanctuary.

Uncompromising Quality

Every product in our collection undergoes rigorous testing and quality control. We partner with world-renowned manufacturers who share our commitment to excellence, ensuring that each piece meets our exacting standards for durability, functionality, and aesthetic appeal.

Sustainable Innovation

We believe luxury and environmental responsibility go hand in hand. Our products incorporate water-saving technologies, sustainable materials, and eco-friendly packaging, demonstrating our commitment to preserving our planet for future generations.

Customer Excellence

Our relationship with customers extends beyond the purchase. Our dedicated team of design consultants provides personalized guidance, ensuring each client finds the perfect pieces to realize their vision of bathroom luxury.
