About Durity

A Decade of Trust. A Legacy of Quality

Our Story

Founded over a decade ago, Durity Bath Fittings has grown into a trusted name in the world of bathroom solutions. What started as a humble initiative to deliver reliable, affordable fittings has now evolved into a respected B2B brand known for its quality, consistency, and innovation.

From day one, our focus has been clear — to build bathroom fittings that are durable, stylish, and engineered for performance. Every Durity product is a reflection of that vision. Designed for modern living and made to withstand time, our fittings are crafted using premium brass, advanced chrome plating, and precision-engineered components that meet rigorous quality standards.

Leak-Proof Performance

Our bathroom accessories are engineered with precision to ensure leak-proof performance, giving you peace of mind and long-lasting reliability. With high-quality seals and carefully tested components, every product is designed to prevent drips and leaks, saving water and protecting your space from potential damage. Whether it’s a faucet, shower head, or pipe fitting, you can count on a flawless seal every time.

Corrosion Resistance

Durability meets elegance in our corrosion-resistant designs. Made from premium materials like stainless steel and brass with advanced coatings, our accessories are built to withstand constant exposure to moisture and humidity. This ensures they retain their strength, shine, and performance even after years of use, making them a smart, low-maintenance investment for your bathroom.

Smooth Water Flow

Experience effortless performance with our accessories designed for smooth water flow. From aerated faucets to precision-engineered showerheads, our products are crafted to deliver consistent, splash-free water flow that feels natural and luxurious. This not only enhances user comfort but also promotes water efficiency—blending performance with sustainability.
